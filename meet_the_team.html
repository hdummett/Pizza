Meet the team

We have a team of the finest chefs and the brainiest inventors here at Pizzeria! Find out more about them below...

  • Grandma

    Founder + Owner

    Grandma learnt the art of pizza-making from her mother whilst growing up in Italy. She founded Molltia Pizzeria in 2002 in the same street we are in today! In her spare time Grandma knits toy pizzas for her 12 grandchildren.

  • Louis Lewis

    Master Chef

    Having worked in Michelin star restaurants in Paris, Hong Kong, and Barcelona, Louis turned his culinary skills to pizza, his first love, when he joined us in 2004.

  • Ramsay Gordon

    Sous-Chef

    Under the close eye of Chef Louis, Ramsay makes all our pizzas to perfection. When he joined us in 2008 he brought with him his famous pulled pork recipe which now features on our BBQ Brussels pizza!

  • Lola Arnold

    Master Pizza Inventor

    Lola invents all of our weird and wacky pizza creations! When not concocting new flavour combinations, Lola plays bass in a punk rock band and snowboards.

  • Marcus Corbusier

    Apprentice Pizza Inventor

    Marcus joined us as an intern in 2012 and has stayed ever since! With a degree in Biochemistry, he enjoys applying science to his experimental new flavours.
